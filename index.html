about me

My names Alec Plummer and I’m a web developer with 3 years experience who specializes in front end development. I have experience in all phases of the development process and have completed, shipped and maintained numerous e-commerce projects throughout my career. I love solving difficult problems with new technologies and enjoy spending my free time expanding my skill set. I’m currently in the process of learning back end via Node, Express and MongoDB.
